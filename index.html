The Design Festival: D'Frost

National Institute of Design R&D Campus, Bengaluru, is back with its annual design and cultural festival, D'Frost '24! This year, we are exploring the various dimensions of design, art, and technology with a lineup of fun activities, curated workshops, and creative department exhibitions.

Whether you're a beginner, an experienced designer, or even a tech enthusiast, there's something for everyone! So what are you waiting for? Join us to celebrate the dimensions of design, and experience D'Frost like never before. Let's travel through dimensions to the world of endless possibilities!

dTalks | Talks

AMIT PATIL
Principal Design Manager

Amit, a Design Leader at Microsoft, shapes Teams & SharePoint experiences. Formerly led Outlook, Bookings, and Viva Insights. Advocate for tech for good. Formerly tackled aviation challenges at Honeywell. Passionate about teaching design and experimenting with methods. Published author of "Oblivion Express." Enjoys Bangalore weather, graphic novels, and family time.

PRATEEK SETHI
Producer | Director | Designer

Founder and Creative Director at Trip Creative Services, a Mumbai and Pune-based award-winning design house. Also an animator, producer, and international speaker. Former host of "FAQ" on Pogo TV | Cartoon Network SEA. Multiple TEDx speaker and Times 40 Under 40 recipient. Producer and Creative Director of "Folktales of India." Active in Animators Guild India. Always seeking innovative ways to engage audiences with 18+ years of experience.

SHWETA RATANAPURA
UX Designer @ Microsoft

In 2018, Shweta excelled in IndiaSkills competitions, representing India at the 45th WorldSkills International Competition in Graphic Design Technology, earning a Bronze Medal. In 2023, she was selected as a volunteer member of the WorldSkills Champions Trust, advocating for vocational skills. With experience at Samsung and Google, Shweta now works as a Designer at Microsoft.

PARAG GUPTA
Sr. UX Designer | Google Cloud

Parag has 14 years of experience working at the intersection of design, engineering, and management. He specializes in growing early-stage products and companies by building strong, process-driven teams that deliver impactful and reliable products. Currently, he leads UX design at Google Cloud. He is also the author of ThePracticalDad.in, a guide to navigating fatherhood. If you see him around, talk to him about databases, board games, and behavioral design.

ATUL KHOLA
Product Designer | Cred

Atul Khola, a graduate of the prestigious National Institute of Fashion Technology in Mumbai, has mastered the art of design. Over the past nine years, he has had the opportunity to work with some of the coolest startups and renowned companies in India and beyond, including Cure.fit, toppr, media.net, and others. Currently, innovation and creativity are being brought to the table by Atul as the lead IC designer at CRED.

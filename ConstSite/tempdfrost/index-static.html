The Design Festival: D'Frost

The National Institute of Design R&D Campus, Bangalore, is back with its sensational annual design and cultural festival, D'Frost 2024! This year, we bring to you a multidimensional experience of immense learning, unbound creativity, and limitless fun. Join us to explore the depths of innovation and creative expression while engaging with multiple disciplines. We are all set to take you on an inter-dimensional journey of design and imagination. Are you ready? Let's travel through dimensions to the world of endless possibilities!
